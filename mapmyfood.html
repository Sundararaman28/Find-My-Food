Idli and Vada These traditional South Indian breakfast dishes made from fermented rice and lentil batter are staples in Chennai. Popular spots to try them include Murugan Idli Shop, Idly Factory, and Idly Express.

Dosa Chennai is renowned for its wide variety of dosas, from the classic masala dosa to creative versions like onion dosa and cheese dosa. Top dosa spots include Murugan Idli Kadai, Rathna Caf√©, and Remix Dosa.

Chennai Filter Coffee The aromatic and flavorful filter coffee, made by brewing dark roasted coffee beans, is a signature beverage of Chennai.

Biryani Biryani, a mixed rice dish made with spices, meat, and vegetables, is a must-try food in Chennai. Recommended places include SS Hyderabadi Biryani, Ambur Star Biryani, and The BVK Biryani.

Chettinad Cuisine Chennai is famous for its spicy and flavorful Chettinad cuisine, which features dishes like chicken Chettinad, mutton kuzhambu, and Chettinad fish fry. Popular restaurants include Savoury Sea Shell, Ayya Chettinad Mess, and Anjappar.

Seafood As a coastal city, Chennai offers a wide range of delectable seafood dishes, such as fish curry, prawn fry, and squid fry. Recommended eateries include Lumarakkon, Savoury Sea Shell, and Zaitoon.

Street food Chennai is renowned for its vibrant street food scene, with specialties like Frankies, Nei Podi Dosa, Bread Omelette, and Kothu Parotta. Popular street food hubs include Sowcarpet, George Town, and Kailash Parbat.

Sweets and Desserts Chennai is also famous for its delectable sweet treats, including Mysore pak, badam halwa, and other traditional desserts.
